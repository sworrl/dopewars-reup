Install Dope Wars: Re-Up

Straight from this page onto your phone. Nothing to download, nothing to unzip.

You're already on an Android phone 🎯

No cable needed — grab the game directly:

Download the game (APK)
Then: open the download and install it
  • Swipe down and tap the finished download, or open it from your Files app.
  • If Android asks, allow "install unknown apps" for your browser — that's normal for games outside the Play Store.
  • Tap Install. Done.

This is an Android game

There's no iOS version (yet). Open this page on an Android phone, or on a computer with your Android phone plugged in.

This browser can't talk to USB devices

The one-click installer needs Chrome or Edge on a computer (Firefox and Safari don't support WebUSB). Two easy options:

  • Open this page in Chrome or Edge and plug your phone in, or
  • Open this page on your Android phone and download the APK directly.
  1. Turn on USB debugging (one-time). Phone: Settings → About phone → tap "Build number" 7×, then Settings → System → Developer options → USB debugging ON.
  2. Plug the phone into this computer with a USB cable and unlock its screen.
  3. Connect — pick your phone in the browser popup, then tap ALLOW on the phone.
  4. Install — we fetch the latest signed release, verify its checksum, and push it to the phone.
Ready when you are.

All set. Unplug the cable and open the game on your phone.

Tip: you can turn USB debugging back off now (Settings → System → Developer options).

My phone isn't listed / nothing happens
  • USB debugging must be ON — see step 1. The phone won't appear in the popup without it.
  • Charge-only cable? Some cables carry no data. Try the cable that came with the phone, or another port.
  • Popup on the phone says "Allow USB debugging?" — tap ALLOW. Tapped Deny? Unplug, replug, it comes back.
  • Windows: if the phone never shows up, install your phone maker's USB driver (search "your brand USB driver").
  • Another tool using the phone? Close Android Studio, adb, or scrcpy — they hog the connection.
